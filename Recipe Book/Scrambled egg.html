Scrambled Egg

instructions

step 1

Stir-fry spring onion and ginger; In a hot pan, add 1 tbsp vegetable oil, spring onion and sliced ginger. Fry for 15 seconds.

step 2

Stir-fry tomatoes and egg; Add tomato and stir fry for 1 min or until slightly soft. Add 4 beaten eggs and 2 tsp of salt. Stir fry until eggs are cooked.

step 3

|| This dish is best eaten as a side dish with steamed white rice.

ingredients

4 eggs(beaten)

1 medium tomato(cut into pieces)

2 spring onion

1 tsp ginger(sliced)

2 stalks coriander(roughly chopped)

2 tsp chili sauce( subsitute with sriracha sauce)

1 tbsp vegetable oil

2 tsp salt
