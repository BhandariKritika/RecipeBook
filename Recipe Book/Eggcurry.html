Egg Curry

instructions

step 1

First boil some potatoes and eggs. After it gets boiled, peel off the skin.

step 2

Chop some onion and tomatoes while the potatoes and eggs are boiling.

step 3

Add some oil in the kadai, and fry the eggs until it becomes golden. Make small cuts in egg before frying.

step 4

Add chopped onions and tomatoes in the kadai (you can use the same oil used to fry egg). And stir it well.

step 5

Add spices like tumeric, masala, red chilli powder, pepper, garlic as you like and stir it well. Then add the boiled potatoes in kadai (You can cut or mash the potatoes) and add salt according to your taste.

step 6

Stir it well and cover it with lid for 1-2mins Add some water and let it boil for 4-5mins. And the egg curry is ready.

ingredients

3-4 potatoes

2 eggs

1 cup chopped onions

2-3 tomatoes

4 tablespoons minced peeled ginger

Salt and black pepper

Other spices as you like
