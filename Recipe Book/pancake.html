Pancakes

instructions

step 1

Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl. Make a well in the center, and pour in milk, oil, and egg. Mix until smooth.

step 2

Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake; cook until it becomes light brown.

step 3

Serve hot and enjoy!

ingredients

1 cup all-purpose flour

2 tablespoons white sugar

2 tablespoons baking powder

1 teaspoon salt, or to your taste

1 cup milk

2 tablespoons vegetable oil

1 egg; beaten
